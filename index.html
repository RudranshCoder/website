About SkillHub

SkillHub is a community-driven platform launched by our college under the Community Engagement Program. Our goal is to connect students with real-world learning experiences through hands-on, practical training. We believe that learning by doing is the best way to prepare for the future.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical expertise, empowering every student with employable skills.

Our Vision

To build a self-sustaining community of learners, mentors, and innovators who create real change through skills and collaboration.
